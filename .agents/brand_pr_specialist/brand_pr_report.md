## Executive Summary

LinkFit is a premier, hyper-local sports matchmaking marketplace and community ecosystem that integrates player discovery, squad organization, instant court booking, split-payment processing, real-time Dynamic Island score tracking, and automated ELO/reliability ratings. 

This comprehensive report details the localized brand launch plan for metropolitan Baku and the broader Azerbaijani market. It delivers production-ready PR copy, a fully structured newsletter strategy with localized email templates, a physical-digital branding blueprint for our flagship luxury venue partner (**Sea Breeze Baku**), and corporate sponsorship models. 

By executing this campaign, LinkFit will establish itself as the digital operating system for amateur sports, starting with Padel Tennis and 5-a-side Football, while driving massive organic user acquisition via viral loop mechanics.

*   **Physical IoT LED Scoreboards ("LinkFit Live Board")**:
    *   *Integration*: We will install physical, wall-mounted digital LED scoreboards at all 6 outdoor padel courts.
    *   *Mechanism*: When players book a court on LinkFit, they receive a unique 4-digit code. Tapping their phone or entering the code on the physical scoreboard syncs the court. During the match, players can adjust the score on the physical board or on their iOS client. The LED board updates instantly, while simultaneously broadcasting live score data to the API, displaying it on the Dynamic Island widgets of remote spectators.
*   **Reception QR & Check-in Stands**:
    *   *Placement*: High-end brushed metal stands placed at the Sea Breeze Padel reception desk and player seating areas.
